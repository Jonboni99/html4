The primary job of a fire engine, also known as a pumper truck, is the transportation of firefighters to an emergency, providing water with which to fight a fire, and carrying other equipment needed by firefighters. A typical fire engine will carry tools for a wide range of firefighting and rescue tasks, along with the ability to respond to medical aid emergencies. Common equipment including ladders, a self-contained breathing apparatus, ventilating equipment, first aid kits, and hydraulic rescue tools.
