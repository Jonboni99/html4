A ladder truck is a truck with a huge hydraulic ladder on top. Ladder trucks are usually called for rescue operations and tall building/warehouse fires. Ladder trucks do not hold water onboard, instead they get their water by connecting to a fire engine, or pumper. Ladder trucks are usually used to perform rescue missions for a victim inside the blaze, or serve as a RIT (Rapid Intervention Team), which is when a firefighter becomes trapped inside the house
