The Fire Trucks of the New York Fire Department

    To Serve, Protect, and Inform

The New York City Fire Department (FDNY), is a department of the government of New York City that provides fire protection, technical rescue, primary response to biological, chemical, and radioactive hazards, and emergency medical services to the city of New York. Throughout this web page are some of the trucks New York Firefighters use to respond to emergencies.
